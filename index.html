Hello.

My name is Jackie Dallas, I’m a retired SSgt. from the United States Air Force. During my 8 year tenure I served as a Radar Technician accumulating unprecedented experience working with high voltage electrical equipment. I became proficient at troubleshooting, isolating electrical malfunctions, reading schematics, component level soldering, signal processing, and using all types of test equipment. I also obtained my Associate's Degree from the Community College of the Air Force in Electronics Systems Technology in 2019. I attended Airman Leadership School and became a frontline supervisor, further diversifying my abilities to not only do hands on maintenance, but also to lead and train others. I received two achievement medals from deployments, and also a commendation medal from one of my former squadrons.
I’m now a full-time student at Codeup Academy, studying to be a Full Stack Web Developer.
